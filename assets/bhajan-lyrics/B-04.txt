
Hari he dayala mora jaya radha-natha
Bara bara ei-bara laha nija satha
				     (Hari)

Bahu yoni bhrami natha lainu sarana
Nija-gune kripa kara adhama-tarana 
				     (Hari)

Jagata-karana tumi jagata-jivana
Toma chada kara nahi he radha-ramana
				     (Hari)

Bhuvana-mangala tumi bhuvanera pati
Tumi upekhshile natha ki haibe gati
				     (Hari)

Bhaviya dekhinu ei jagata-majhare
Toma vina keho nahi e dase uddhare
				     (Hari)
