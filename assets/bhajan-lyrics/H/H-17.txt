Ramana Manjari Ashtakam

aharniśam bhāvanāmṛtāyām bhāvitā
bhūtvā vismarayasi tvam laukika guṇagāthā
tattadbhāva bardhanārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

pāṭ alavastra campakadyuti manoharam
tvam śrīrādhikāyāḥ abhisāra tava nityakāma
abhisāra kāmārthaṁ tvam kalpavallari
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

kṛṣṇā kṛṣṇa niśā yogya śṛṅgāra kṛtvā
abhisārārthaṁ kṛtvā kḷpta karpūrabhūṣā
śrīrādhā–abhisārārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

niśā yām pathi vṛkṣaṁ dṛṣṭvā muṇḍita muṇḍam
vivarṇa bhaviṣyati śrīrādhāyāḥ tuṇḍam
rādhābhisārārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

abhisāra kāle tava skandhe muditā nyastahastā
guravaḥ pratāranārthaṁ śiñjit (tvayā) vastravṛtā
guravaḥ pratāranārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

abhisāra śikṣā yārthaṁ kaṇṭaka kardamākta prāṅgaṇe
śrīrādhāṁ cālayaṭi (ati) mṛdu saṁgopane
abhisāra śikṣā yārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

atyukaṭa māna prasādhanārthaṁ śrīrādhāyāḥ
apekṣayati yukti sadā suvalojjvalayoḥ
rādhā–mānotkarṣārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

jitvā vrajarāja sūnum vākyudghe
śrīrāseśvaryyaḥ kṛpā labhasi gaṇamadhye
rāseśvaryyaḥ kṛpārthaṁ tvam kalpavallạri
kṛpā kadā kariṣyasi mām śrīramaṇa mañjari

itīmamadbhuta navīna stotraṁ 
niśamya śrīramaṇa mañjari
lalitāyāḥ gaṇe sthānamm datvā
kariṣyasi nitya sahacari
