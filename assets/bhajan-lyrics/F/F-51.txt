Oh, I have forgotten, I have forgotten about many things.
I have forgotten about my millions of births.
I have forgotten my relation to Krishna!

Because of the covering of maya, I have forgotten all my previous births and deaths. Thinking that I am this body, I have forgotten the splendid beauty of Krishna who was born in Gokul!

I have taken birth in this earth with other sinful people like me who is running after wealth and fame. Due to the pride in me, I have forgotten the enchanting flute song from Krishna!

I have forgotten about that boy from Gokul who takes all the cows to herd. Oh, I have forgotten about Him to whom all the gopis run, sing His glories and dance with Him.

He is called Radha Madhava who is extremely beautiful. He is our dear Kesava who has no beginning but born as a son to Nandababa. I have forgotten this hero who is dressed like a dancer and performs nectarean pastimes.
