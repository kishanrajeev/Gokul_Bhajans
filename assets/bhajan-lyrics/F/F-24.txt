 -------------- Meaning -------
O gurudeva! you came to save the world.
You bestow the greatest treasure the bhakti.
I surrender at your holy lotus feet.
O gurudeva! You are the captain of my boat.
 
You drive away my fear of birth and death.
You drive away my worldly illusions.
You drive away my material desires.
O gurudeva! You are the captain of my boat.

You are dearest to the dearmost of Radha Raman.
You are wonderfully serving Them in their leelas.
You are the personification of mercy.
O gurudeva! You are the captain of my boat.
