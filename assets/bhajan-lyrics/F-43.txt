------------ meaning ----------

O Krishna! O the one who has beautiful lotus like eyes, You are so fond of hearing melodious songs and bhajans (sangeetam).

Your charming nectarian face attracts everyone's mind. You are the original cupid who killed madhu demon.

Vrindavan dham is Your abode. You steal away the hearts and minds of all the vraja vasis (gopis).

You are the one who was holding the Govardhan hill (with Your finger). You are govinda and Lord Hari Hara.
