                             ... (anupama)
Chavīlī hai adā bāki 
Sughara-su rata madhura batiyā
Lataka gardana kī mana basiyā
Hamāre śyāma-śyāmā kī  	     ... (anupama)

Mukuta aur candrika māthe, 
Adhara par pāna kī lālī
Aho kai si-bani chavi hai, 
Hamāre śyāma-śyāmā kī  	     ... (anupama)
 
Parasa para mili ke do phāre, 
Srī vṛndāvan kī kunjana meń
Nahi bara nata bana śobhā, 
Hamāre śyāma-śyāmā kī	     ... (anupama)
 
Nahi kucha lālasā dhana kī, 
Nahi nir māna kī icchā
Sakhī, śyāma kī do daraśan, 
Dayā ho śyāma-śyāmā kī	     ... (anupama)

-------------------------------------------------
1) The sweetness of the divine couple our
Syama-Syama is unprecedented! Their eyes are 
so full of rasa - Oh, our Syama Syama!

2) Their charming expressions and gestures,
Their beautiful faces, Their sweet talking,
Their necks gently tilted toward each other,
this site all dwell in my heart, Oh Syama Syama!
 
3) Their foreheads are adorned with crowns and
a special ornament (Candrika), and Their lips
are red with tampula. Oh, what a wonderful
sight - Oh, our Syama Syama!
 
4) I have no words to describe Their grandeur when
They meet and enjoy Their pastimes in the kunjas of
Sri Vrindavana - Oh, our Syama Syama!
 
5) I do not have any longing for wealth or any
desire for liberation. Be merciful and give this
Syama Sakhi Your darsana, Oh, our Syama Syama!
