Jaya rādha ramanā! jaya sri hari govindā 
Jaya hari sri hari gopal, jaya jaya sri nāthā   (Jaya)
 
Yasomati nandanā sri hari, yamunā nata chāri
Ananda rupa syama! ananda sa-yi sayanā          (Jaya)
 
Pankaja lochanā sri hari, peetāmbara dhāri 
Bhuvana mohana rama!  brindāvana dhāmā          (Jaya)
 
Yamunā jeevanā sri hari, vrinda vana-māli  
Yadhukula varadā krishna! vamsi vata nayanā     (Jaya)
 
Sita nāyakā sri hari, sarva bhaya hāri
Syāma sundara rupa, jaya jaya sri krishna!      (Jaya)
