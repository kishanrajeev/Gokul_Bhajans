Raadhikaa Krishna Raadhikaa
tava virahe keshava, 
sarasam-srnnamapi malayaja pankam 
pasyati vishhameva vapusshisa sankham

Svasita pavanam anupama parinaham
madana dahanam iva vahati sadaa aham
disi disi tirati sajala kanaaalam
nayana nalinam iva vigalita naalam  .. (Raadhikaa)
 
nayana vishhayam  api kisalaya talpam
gannayati vihita hutaasa vikalpam
tyajati na paanni talena kapolam
baala sasinam iva saayam alolam      .. (Raadhikaa)
