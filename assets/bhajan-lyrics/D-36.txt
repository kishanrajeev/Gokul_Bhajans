-------------------Meaning -----------
O Kanha, Hear me before you go. Krishna just tell me why you are so black? 
And even after being so black, why are you different from this world?

I was born on a black night and I drank the milk of a black cow. 
My blanket is also black, and hence I'm so black.

I danced on a black snake and I conquered a black snake. 
The snakes have black color, and hence I'm so black.

Everyday, Gopis call me in their house and feed me butter and sugar. 
Gopis have a black mind, and hence I'm so black.

Gopis put kājal in their eyes and I am the apple of their eye. 
The color of the kājal is black, and hence I'm so black.
